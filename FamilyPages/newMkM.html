About Madanpur Family

Madanpur has had a glorious past and the Mathurs of this erstwhile 'estate' have been instrumental in maintaining and preserving the valuable traditions of their forefathers. Madanpur family commonly called as (Madanpur key Mathur in Hindi i.e. Mathurs of Madanpur) has registered its ownership with Thakur Shree Raj Gopalji Maharaj and kept its roots and structure strong. Our Forefathers gave us the temple and address which has been our flagship and identity at all times.
