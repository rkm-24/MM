About Madanpur Family

Madanpur has had a glorious past and the Mathurs of this erstwhile 'estate' have been instrumental in maintaining and preserving the valuable traditions of their forefathers. Madanpur family commonly called as (Madanpur key Mathur in Hindi i.e. Mathurs of Madanpur) has registered its ownership with Thakur Shree Raj Gopalji Maharaj and kept its roots and structure strong. Our Forefathers gave us the temple and address which has been our flagship and identity at all times.

The bonding of our 'khandaan' is reflected when the family congregates to rejoice the 'mela' of Shree Raj Gopalji Maharaj, annually during the auspicious month 'Chaitra' of Hindu calendar. This khandaan is enriched by enthusiasm, energy and spark of youth, where the experience and wisdom of elders has keept alive its traditions, celebrations, temple customs and culture as heritage.

Sharing is caring, when we as family share memories, narrate amusing anecdotes, meet and greet with enthusiasm for celebrating and taking pride in the achievements of our extended family, the Madanpur khandaan. To be a member of Madanpur family is a blessing like sunshine in winter morning. Formality which contracts conversations to monosyllables is not there when Madanpur stirs a million chords, vibrant laughters being with the family and relatives devoid of any divide of status, position, power etc.

This buoyancy of togetherness at Madanpur 'mela' has kept the family bonds strong.

Digital Presence

Global presence is the reality of today's world, yet a need to remain rooted and connected is required for self-esteem. Our talents, expertise, forums, culture, ethics can be integrated/associated with a digital presence and connectivity.

We know anecdotes of our family, our parents and grand-parents had resided at Madanpur and most of them shifted to Shikohabad or other nearby areas. They narrated and we heard them and a nostalgic fragrance remains with us throughout the life. Some relatives and names are familiar but exact connection is not clear as in the drift with the tides of time the bonding could not be sustained in so many cases.

We need the keys to open the doors which seem to have closed with time due to several issues like distances from Madanpur (our roots) due to work, busy life, travel difficulties or similar reasons. There is a strong need to re-establish the lost ties and bring families together again on a common platform improving relations through communication.

It is said “If there is a will, there is a way.”This digital platform is to draw together our family. Various activities have been added to generate interest of all age groups and we remain open to add many more.

Making a website is an outcome of this thought. The name of website is kept keeping our ancestors and khandaan in mind and to align it with family tree in some way. (family tree will be added soon)

How we got this idea?

Most of us are already using various social media available today. This website will connect close relatives of Madanpur family. This will add many in the family and with everyone's cooperation it will become our main forum to interact and link with the family. This thought inspired us to create this website when our family members make and develop this family website our private information (data) will remain safe within the family.

What is the road ahead?

We have a plan to introduce a dynamic online family tree to be updated by the registered members and understand the links and relations.

The website should involve and include ideas of all ages to make this site interesting. This website will be the place for members to introduce themselves and form groups as per their talents, hobbies, creative skills, achievements.

There may be competitions, quiz and other engaging activities in future based on the request of members.

So the forum is open for members and sky is the limit for the vision.

Our Teams

We have planned to make various teams for various activities of 'haveli', temple and website. We encourage the initiative and involvement, to perform and get recognised.

The teams will be responsible for routine and innovative activities in the sections as mentioned below;

  • 1- Web section: Design, development, hosting, uploading and keep it alive,
  • 2- Content section: Content writing, edit and update,
  • 3- Talent-section: collection and finalising to publish,
  • 4- Images and videos sections: collection and finalising display
  • 5- Feedback and Communication section: Read/respond to suggestions, comments and mails accordingly etc.

  • More over better form of management is expected. Over and above this everyone will communicate and motivate others to join the site and give suggestions/comments and participate to improve the site.
